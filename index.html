Analyzing the Recent Increase in Book Bans in Schools

Caitlin, Deepak, & Jiwon

The last several months have seen a rapid, unprecedented increase in the ban of books across the United States. This recent banning has been known to target and censor books by or about people of underrepresented communities such as the LGBTQ+ community and people of color, but there have not been many extensive visualizations that shed light on the significant impact of book bans in more detail.
